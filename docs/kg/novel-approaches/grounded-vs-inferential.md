Taxonomy: claims split into grounded (matches single evidence chunk) vs inferential (synthesizes across papers). Only grounded claims scored for correctness. Inferential claims evaluated on directional support. Chunk-level matching for honest grounding rates. Novel for multi-document synthesis where cross-paper claims are inherently harder to ground.
